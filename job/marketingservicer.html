Marketing Services Manager

Job type: Full time
Mode: Onsite/remote
Location: Ibadan

Job Summary

We are looking for a detail-oriented individual to join our team as the Marketing Services Manager. The responsibilities of the Marketing Services Manager include to oversee the operations of digital marketing team, planning clients’ marketing projects, delegating tasks to project team members, supervising those employees, and ensuring marketing projects are completed on time and within budget. This position will partner and build strong relationships with clients, creative team members and outside vendors. This position is regarded as a trusted advisor and works closely with the clients, designers and marketing managers to set project milestones, create project timelines and track schedules to ensure all deliverable deadlines are met on time and on brand.

To be successful in this role, there should be in-depth knowledge of digital media, marketing and creative workflows. The Marketing Services Manager should also have experience executing digital marketing projects from conception to production, including event design projects to ultimately, ensure that our creative promotes the clients’ brands through delivering high quality on tight deadlines.

Key Responsibilities:

In this role, the Marketing Services Manager will be

  • Monitor the production of all jobs, ensuring priorities are consistent with commitments and production decisions are cost effective actions to achieve results
  • Entering, updating and closing projects in project management system
    • Client Management
  • Work with teams such as Corporate Branding, Product Marketing, Content Marketing, Demand Generation, Learning and Development, Field Marketing, and Marketing Operations on intake and project management on all creative projects
  • Facilitate consultation with project stakeholders and creative talent on branding, design, content, and technical needs and counsel internal clients on best practices and approaches for process and budgeting
  • Schedule projects and define budget constraints
  • Share technical expertise with project teams to help define scope, schedule, and manage expectations for all creative projects
    • Project Management
  • Manage project resource planning, reporting, and assignment
  • Coordinate internal project resources and analyze staffing requirements to ensure project timeliness
  • Plan duties, coordinate, and assist in the definition of project scope to ensure work is completed efficiently and effectively
  • Communicate professionally with all teams; maintain communication channels for escalation issues
  • Report and escalate to management as needed regarding resource and project escalations
Job Requirements:
  • Bachelor degree in a discipline related to marketing with 3-5 years of relevant work experience OR demonstrated ability to meet the job requirements through a comparable number of years of applicable work experience
  • Ad agency, technology, SaaS, and/or marketing industry experience preferred
  • Strong process management skills and commitment to continuously improving the quality and timeliness of his/her own work and that of group and department
  • Require knowledge of or ability to quickly learn specialized software to complete work assignments, such as Marketing Planning Software (preference for Wrike)
  • Able to demonstrate continued expertise in the primary tools or applications used in completing daily work assignments
  • Strong organizational, time management, and adaptability skills
  • Team player with strong interpersonal skills, specifically building relationships with cross-functional teams
  • Exceptional attention to detail and ability to maintain information
  • Ability to work independently and take initiative to resolve questions
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Creative problem-solving
  • Experience with developing schedules and timelines for multiple projects
  • Project management knowledge and experience a plus

Job Type: Full-time

Salary: Attractive.
