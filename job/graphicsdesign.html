Digital Media Associate (Graphics Design)

Job type: Full time
Mode: Onsite/remote
Location: Ibadan

Job Summary

We are seeking a skilled, experienced Graphic Designers to join our growing team. In this position, they will shape and create brand imagery and visuals story (motion and stills) for our clients through highly engaging, interactive, impactful, and result-oriented visual campaigns. They will be responsible for Graphic designs (motion and stills), animations with all related activities as well as the visual (look & feel) coordination of assigned campaigns for all brands in their respective portfolio.

Must have passion for social media and up-to-date knowledge of the latest social media developments and trends. Must have knowledge and experience of using Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and YouTube, and excellent time management and organizational skills.

Key Responsibilities
  • Study design briefs and determine requirements
  • Conceptualize visuals based on requirements -Prepare rough drafts and present ideas
  • Develop illustrations, logos and other designs using software or by hand
  • Use the appropriate colours and layouts for each graphic
  • Work with copywriters and account managers to produce final design
  • Test graphics across various media
  • Amend designs after feedback
  • Ensure final graphics and layouts are visually appealing and on-brand
  • Any other task not found inconsistent with this role
Requirements:
  • Bachelor's Degree in Graphic Arts, Design, or any related discipline.
  • 2 - 3 years experience in a fast-paced marketing agency
  • Passion for (talent building, ability to write) visualize strategy and plans will also be an added advantage
  • Digital marketing skills with minimum ability to perform activities on the web, tweaking website visual themes, Google Advertising, IG, Facebook Business Manager, LinkedIn Ads, Email Marketing and SEM
  • Superior understanding of Nigerian creative landscape and global industry trends.
  • Experience with the use of graphic and digital tools.
  • Experience with visual creation and deployment across all Social Media platforms
  • Certification in Digital marketing, illustration, and animation will be an added advantage.

Job Type: Full-time

Salary: ₦50,000.00 – ₦80,000.00 per month
