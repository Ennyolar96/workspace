What is coworking?

+
  • Coworking is a global movement that provides collaborative and innovative office space solutions for people to grow their businesses. It is generally defined as flexible, membership-based workspaces where diverse groups of freelancers, remote workers, and other independent professionals work together in a shared, communal setting.

How can I book a workspace?

+
  • Before you can book for a workspace either online or onsite, you need to first register to obtain your membership ID through Register button on https://workspace.marketleag.com. After your membership is confirmed, you would need to login to your account where you would be able to make booking.
  • Along with you booking request, you would need to make payment to the account details that pops up with your booking information when you click Confirm button on the booking page, and send your evidence of payment to mypay4space@marketleag.com.
  • Note: your payment needs to be confirmed before your booking would be scheduled.
